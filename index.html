Chosen disorder: Gestational Diabetes (also known as Gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM)

As a pregnant woman, it is pertinent for to have certain health information. Undoubtedly, this information is vital for the overall health of both the mother and the baby. A pregnant woman needs to know about a condition known as Gestational Diabetes.

What does this disorder mean? It is a condition in which a pregnant woman without diabetes develops high blood sugar levels during the period of the pregnancy. Gestational diabetes mellitus according to Keenan-Lindsay (2020), GDM is not a permanent disorder as glucose level mostly returns to normal around six weeks after delivery (p. 109).
